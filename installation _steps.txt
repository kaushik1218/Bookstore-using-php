Step 1: Create a database name: "book_store1"
step 2: import "BookStoreCreator.sql" & InsertBookInventory.sql file from database folder
Step 3: Register yourself using register page
Step 4: once you register please sign in
Stpe 5: Please select a book from BookStore in the menu
Step 6: Add a book to the cart
Step 7: Select quantity you want to buy and click on Re-calculate to update the bookinventory in db with updated quantity
Step 8: Now click on 'Confirm & Proceed'
Step 9: You will be taken to Payment page. Fill the form and submit. Your order ID will be generated.
